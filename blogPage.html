Introducing Myself: A Journey from Digital Marketing to Web Development

Upasana

May 03,2024

Hello! My name is Upasana, and I am thrilled to share my journey with you. I began my professional career in the dynamic field of digital marketing, and now I am venturing into the world of web development as I pursue my studies at Humber College.

My Digital Marketing Background

My foray into digital marketing began a few years ago, driven by a passion for understanding consumer behavior and a knack for creativity. I enjoyed crafting compelling content, strategizing social media campaigns, and analyzing data to drive business growth. Working in digital marketing allowed me to blend my creative skills with analytical thinking, making each project a unique challenge.

Transitioning to Web Development

As much as I enjoyed digital marketing, I found myself increasingly fascinated by the technical aspects of the web. I wanted to understand the "how" behind the websites I was promoting and explore the intricate world of coding and development. This curiosity led me to Humber College, where I am now pursuing a diploma in web development.
